Game Cafe

After four weeks of HTML, CSS and JavaScript we were given four days to complete our first project. This was partly to evaluate how we were getting on by the middle of the course and partly to finally give us complete creative control to see what we could come up with and to build a bit of confidence in our newly acquired abilities. At least, that was my understanding of it. I may have made up some of that reasoning. For my project I made a web site for a gaming café that let people book seats at a game event. This was all made by hand on VS Code using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and utilising local storage.
